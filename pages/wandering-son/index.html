Overview

Wandering Son is a manga and anime series about several trans children as they grow up in school.

Nitori

Nitori

Nitori, the main character of the series and namesake of this entire website, is a trans girl. The story overall is an arc about her understanding and coming to terms with her identity.

Takatsuki

Takatsuki

Takatsuki is Nitori's classmate and friend. He is a trans boy [1].
